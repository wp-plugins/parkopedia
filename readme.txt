The Parkopedia plugin let's you embed an interactive parking map onto your website, helping your users find parking around your location.

== Description ==

Add a free parking map to your website! Help your visitors find you while also helping them to find parking near you!

It is super easy to use with a shortcode. This is the perfect map for your contact page or to enhance your existing Google Maps. There are currently over 38 million parking spaces listed all around the world.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `parkopedia.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Embed your parking map using our shortcode: [parkopedia lat="51.5078238802915" lng="-0.09417821970849796" width="800" height="500" title="London Parking"]
